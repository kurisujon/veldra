# Brand Guidelines

## Brand Voice
- **Professional**: Trustworthy, reliable, and competent.
- **Empathetic**: Understanding the high stakes of visa applications and document verification.
- **Clear**: Direct and unambiguous. No jargon.

## Visual Identity
- **Logo**: Simple, typographic or minimalist icon.
- **Aesthetic**: Clean, structured, and warm. Avoid dark mode as the default; prioritize warm white, paper-like environments.
- **Imagery**: If used, should be abstract or highly professional line art. Avoid generic stock photos of people pointing at screens.

## Do's and Don'ts
- **DO**: Use simple, reassuring language.
- **DO**: Make the interface feel like a well-organized digital desk.
- **DON'T**: Use "AI" magic sparkles or overly futuristic aesthetics.
- **DON'T**: Overwhelm the user with bright, saturated colors.
