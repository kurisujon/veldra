# Product Vision: Veldra

## Overview
Veldra is a Smart Document Verification Platform focused on student visa application document checking. Our goal is to streamline the meticulous process of reviewing educational and personal documents, ensuring compliance and accuracy while significantly reducing manual effort.

## Core Objective
To provide document staff, HR professionals, and application reviewers with a clean, comfortable, and reliable tool that highlights discrepancies across complex document sets, while generating necessary legal or explanatory drafts seamlessly.

## Target Audience
- Document Verification Staff
- HR Professionals
- University Admissions Teams
- Student Visa Application Consultants

## Key Value Propositions
- **Human-Centered Review**: A calm, minimalist interface designed for long review sessions.
- **Intelligent Verification**: Automated detection of inconsistencies across various document types.
- **Actionable Outputs**: Instant generation of discrepancy affidavits and explanation letters.
